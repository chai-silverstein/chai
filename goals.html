My Goals in Pursuing a Career in Software Development

  • Why do I want to learn these skills?
    1. I admire people who can take logic and art and create something useful.
    2. I want to have a flexible career.
    3. I find myself working jobs that have steep learning curves but no room for personal growth.
    4. I can't wait to find an environment of like-minded people so that we may grow together.

  • How will this skill change my life and my career?
    1. Ultimately I would like to find a job that allows for creativity and collaboration.
    2. I hopefully will have the freedom to travel more with my husband.
    3. I want to be able to work on a variety of projects and challenge myself in the process.

  • What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?
    1. I would love to find a workplace with similar values to my own and an engaging culture that promotes growth, collaboration, and creativity.
    2. Ideally, I would be working remotely so that I may travel freely.
    3. My husband and I could collaborate on projects and I would finally understand his "tech babble."
    4. I would be able to build and add flair to my favorite puzzle games like Sudoku and Binary Dots.
